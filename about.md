# DigiCloset

## What It Is

DigiCloset is an AI-powered commerce product for Shopify merchants. It helps fashion and apparel brands improve product discovery, increase shopper confidence, and lift conversion through virtual try-on, AI-generated outfit recommendations, and merchant-facing optimization tools.

The product combines:

- A storefront try-on widget for shoppers
- A merchant admin experience for onboarding, pricing, and launch readiness
- Backend APIs for try-on generation, recommendations, billing, and webhooks
- AI and inference services for image generation, matching, and asynchronous processing

## Core Value Proposition

DigiCloset helps merchants turn static product pages into interactive shopping experiences.

Instead of asking shoppers to imagine how a garment might look, DigiCloset gives them a more visual, more confident path to purchase through:

- Virtual try-on previews
- AI-assisted outfit suggestions
- Better product engagement on PDPs
- Merchant tools to manage rollout and quality

## Product Surfaces

### 1. Storefront Widget

The Shopify widget is embedded on product pages and is designed to feel premium, simple, and conversion-friendly.

It supports:

- Customer image upload
- Garment image upload
- Try-on generation flow
- Progress states and polling
- Result display
- Download, share, and add-to-cart style actions

### 2. Merchant Admin Experience

The admin UI is built to guide a merchant from installation to launch.

It includes:

- Overview dashboard
- Launch onboarding
- Pricing plans
- Navigation and guided setup flows
- Launch-readiness messaging and support prompts

### 3. Backend + AI Services

The backend and microservices architecture supports:

- Shopify app integration
- Billing and credit-aware operations
- Webhooks and compliance handling
- Try-on job orchestration
- Async processing and status polling
- Recommendation and optimization capabilities

## Key Capabilities

- AI-powered virtual try-on
- AI outfit and recommendation generation
- Shopify embedded app architecture
- Async generation pipeline
- Merchant onboarding and launch flows
- Billing and plan support
- GDPR and webhook support
- Analytics and optimization foundations

When generating ads or copy, prefer claims like:

- "helps increase shopper confidence"
- "designed to improve conversion potential"
- "built for fashion merchants on Shopify"
- "turns static product pages into interactive shopping experiences"

Avoid hard numeric claims unless the numbers are verified and approved.
